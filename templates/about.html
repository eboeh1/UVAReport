About This Application

Basic information about our application and the differences between UVA Report and existing resources.

Our Application

UVA Report is a whistleblower application used to report academic fraud with a focus on class design rather that just reporting your peers. We have noticed that many people are irritated by cheating that occurs at UVA, but few want to be responsible for the consequences of their peers. This site emphasizes reporting broad cases of cheating that have been observed by students. UVA report wants professors to be aware of how cheating it happening so they can design that class in a way to prevent cheating in the future.

Our Inspiration

After interviewing our peers at UVA about their current feelings about the honor system, we quickly realized hardly any students feel comfortable reporting an honor offense. We discovered the root of this discomfort was often due to the fact that students feared retaliation from their peers if their name was attatched to a report, and could not solely be attributed to a lack of desire to report these issues. This realization sparked our group to create this whistleblower application to fufill this unmet need.

Should I report anonymously?

UVA Honor Report intentionally allows the user to decide whether to sign into their Google account or to report the witnessed offense anonymously. We encourage students to sign into their google accounts so that Honor officials are able to properly follow up on the report and find a solution to the issue. Additionally, signing in allows users to retain access to their submitted forms and see the status of their report, providing users with more transparency about the review process.

However, we understand the importance of an anomyous outlet that allows students the option of reporting an offense without their name attatched. An anonymous report is better than no report, so if students are truly uncomfortable reporting from their Google account, we also encourage the use of this anonymous reporting feature.
