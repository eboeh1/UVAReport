Honor information

If you need to file a real honor report at UVA, here are existing resources that you can use.

UVA Honor Contact Page

UVA Honor has a report page that allows you to directly contact the honor committee. If you want to make a report that goes directly to the honor committee, please follow the link to the Honor Committee contact page.

Time Sensitive Reports

If you have time sensitive concerns, please email honor@virginia.edu or call (434)-924-7438.

You can also view the information for each school's honor representitive here.

Just Report It

UVA's current online reporting application, Just Report It., is meant to allow students to report issues such as bias, discrimination based on gender, race, or sexuality, and hazing. If your Honor report is of this nature, using this form instead of the Honor Committee contact page will help ensure that you can access resources beyond UVA Honor in a timely manner.

What's Next?

After a report is submitted, there are three more steps that the Honor Council takes before any consequences occur. This includes an Investigation, a Hearing, and finally a Sanctioning. The Reporter is not necessarily involved in the rest of this process. However, assuming the Reporter includes their name with their submission, the Honor council will reach out with any outstanding questions. Our application will display the status of reports to the Reporter as they go through this process.
